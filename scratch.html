BYU Women's Chorus

Welcome to my scratch page! I am a member of BYU Women's Chorus at BYU, and I love the music I get to perform there. BYU Women's Chorus is a large ensemble comprised of more than 180 singers. The choir is conducted by Dr. Sonja Poulter, who has been directing the choir since 2019 as an adjunct BYU faculty member.
